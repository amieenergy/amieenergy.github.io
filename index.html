The Barre with Amie Energy

The Barre with Amie Energy is a unique boutique fitness studio located in the new Quarterbank container precinct of New Plymouth’s City Centre. Classes fuse principals of ballet barre, Pilates, cardio and strength training. Using biomechanics and music it is both effective and oh so fun.

Like the name suggests most of the class is done at a ballet style barre however there is some freestanding and mat work. It includes a variety of equipment: stability balls, flex-bands and hand weights.

No dance experience is required. With five class types on offer the studio caters for teens to more mature ages and both men and women. Please refer to timetable for class descriptions.

Why is it good?

Targeting all muscle groups, it creates that aspiring dancer’s physique – leaner, toned, stronger and longer looking. It’s softer on your joints than most traditional types of exercise.

Benefits of barre with Amie Energy are:
• Connect with your mind and heart through tailored music
• Increased energy and body awareness
• Toned arms, back, butt and thighs
• Flat abdomen
• Elongated muscles
• Increased strength and stamina
• Improved bone density
• Assistance with improved digestion, anxiety and stress levels
• Connection to a new health and wellness community

Why Amie Energy?

Amie Hine - Owner and Principal Barre Teacher was a previous dance teacher in New Plymouth prior to completing a degree in Marketing and Management at Victoria University, Wellington. She has lived overseas for some 18 years in London and Sydney, Australia. It was whilst living in Sydney that she found her passion for barre and the connection with her childhood talents.

“After being a ‘gym junkie’ for years’ it was refreshing to find something new that I loved but also gave me results: mind and body – Bingo!”

Her certification is globally recognised, STOTT Pilates Total Barre – Merrithews premium brand (based out of Canada).
