Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I attend a class to achieve results?

By attending two classes per week you can significantly increase strength and flexibility and feel overall body tone. Like most forms of exercise, greater results are achieved from greater frequency and consistency on going. Most clients should see and feel results in as little as ten classes.

Is there a change room / bathroom / shower?

There is a bathroom on site which is available as a change are. There is no shower facility.

Is there parking available?

There is a public carpark opposite the studio entry is via Powderham Street (see location information for a map) this is only $1.00 / hour. There is also free street parking just a few streets away.

How do I pay for my class?

To guarantee your spot at THE BARRE it is essential to book and purchase in advance. Current method is via email or phone. We accept walk-ins subject to availability. We also accept cash or band transfer in the studio.

What if I need to cancel?

Please email or phone to cancel. If you don’t attend or cancel within ten hours of the class time you will be charged for the class.

How do I know when I’m suitable for Barre Attack classes?

Depending on fitness and background some clients may be able to start on Barre Attack. There is no rule on the number of other barre classes you need to attend before moving up to this class type. To be sure it is best to ask Amie Energy for guidance.
